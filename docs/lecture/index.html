Lecture Notes

These notes are based on the MIT OpenCourseWare video lectures given in July 1986 by Hal Abelson and Gerald Jay Sussman. They follow the first edition (1985) of Structure and Interpretation of Computer Programs, so there are some differences from the textbook notes which follow the second edition (1996).
